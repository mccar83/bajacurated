SAFETY POLICY

Requirements to Participate

  • Valid driver's license required for the primary driver of each vehicle.
  • Minimum age: 18 years to drive. Passengers must be at least 6 years old.
  • Participants must be in good physical health. Please inform us of any medical conditions prior to the experience.
  • Pregnant women are not permitted to participate in off-road experiences.
  • Maximum weight per vehicle seat: 130 kg (286 lbs).

Safety Gear

  • Helmet, goggles, gloves, and neck brace are provided and mandatory for all participants.
  • Closed-toe shoes are required. Sandals and flip-flops are not permitted.
  • Each vehicle is equipped with a radio for real-time communication with the guide.

During the Experience

  • All participants must follow the guide's instructions at all times.
  • Alcohol consumption before or during the experience is strictly prohibited.
  • Maintaining a safe distance from other vehicles is mandatory at all times.
  • Speed limits set by the guide must be respected on all terrain types.
  • Baja Curated reserves the right to end the experience if a participant endangers the group.

Vehicles

  • All Can-Am vehicles are 2025/2026 models, factory maintained, fully insured, and inspected before each experience.
  • Participants are responsible for damages caused by reckless or negligent driving.
By joining a Baja Curated experience, all participants acknowledge and accept these safety conditions. A liability waiver must be signed on site prior to departure.

CANCELLATION POLICY

Client Cancellations

  • More than 48 hours before the experience: full refund.
  • Between 24 and 48 hours before: 50% refund or full credit toward a future date.
  • Less than 24 hours before or no-show: no refund.

Rescheduling

  • Rescheduling is available at no cost with at least 24 hours notice, subject to availability.
  • Each booking can be rescheduled a maximum of one time.

Weather & Force Majeure

  • If Baja Curated cancels due to extreme weather or safety conditions, clients receive a full refund or priority reschedule.
  • Light rain or wind does not qualify as a cancellation condition.

Group Bookings

  • Groups of 5 or more require a 30% deposit at the time of booking.
  • Partial cancellations within a group are subject to the same policy.

How to Cancel or Reschedule

Contact us via WhatsApp or email with your booking details. We will confirm within 24 hours.

All refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to the original payment method. For questions, contact us at info@bajacurated.com or WhatsApp +52 (55) 2900-7905.
