Why Baja Curated

THE DIFFERENCE

🎮
You Drive — Not a Staff Member

This is the critical difference. Most 'private' UTV tours in Cabo are ride-alongs — a staff driver operates the vehicle. At Baja Curated, you control the Can-Am. Your guide leads the route; you drive it.

👥
Exclusive Group Only

Private means private. Your booking is never combined with other guests. One guide, one group, one departure. The route pace, the stops, and the breaks are yours to decide.

🏆
Authorized BRP Dealer Fleet

Every Can-Am in our fleet is current-year, purchased from BRP, and maintained at our certified workshop. Not a third-party rental. Not a worn machine. A warrantied 2025/2026 Maverick every time.

📡
In-Vehicle & Inter-Vehicle Radios

Two-way radio communication inside your Can-Am (driver to passenger) and between all vehicles in your convoy. Your guide is always a voice away regardless of terrain or distance.

🛡
Full Safety Gear Included

BRP helmet, harness, neck brace, goggles, and gloves — included on every tour. Safety briefing before departure. All vehicles are fully insured and inspected before each ride.

💰
Price Per Vehicle, Not Per Person

Each Can-Am fits 2 passengers. The tour price covers the vehicle — if two people share, the per-person cost is half the listed price. Groups with multiple vehicles get the full private convoy experience.

FAQ

COMMON QUESTIONS

Do I actually drive in a private UTV tour with Baja Curated?+
Yes — you are the driver. This distinguishes Baja Curated from the majority of UTV tour operators in Cabo San Lucas, who operate ride-along experiences where a staff member drives the vehicle and guests are passengers. At Baja Curated, a valid driver's license, minimum age 18, and the safety briefing are the only requirements to get behind the wheel.
What does 'private' mean for UTV tours in Cabo?+
At Baja Curated, private means your group is never combined with strangers. One guide is dedicated exclusively to your group. Departure time, route pace, and stop frequency are adjusted to your preferences. No shared vehicles, no caravan with unfamiliar guests, no compromise on the experience.
What UTV vehicles are used on private tours in Cabo?+
All tours operate on 2025/2026 Can-Am Maverick UTVs — Trail, DS Max, and Maverick R models. Baja Curated is an Authorized Can-Am BRP Dealer, meaning these are not rental units but certified dealer vehicles with full manufacturer warranty and authorized service.
How many people fit in one UTV for a private Cabo tour?+
Each Can-Am Maverick holds 2 passengers (driver + 1). For groups larger than 2, additional vehicles are added — each driven by a member of your group. The guide leads in the front vehicle. All vehicles maintain radio contact throughout the route.
What private UTV tour routes are available near Cabo San Lucas?+
Six routes are available: Desert Intro 3H (desert trails, SJC — $550), Explorer 6H (Palo Escopeta, Los Frailes, Cabo Pulmo coast — $950), Cabo Pulmo Expedition (UNESCO reef + snorkel — $1,595), Challenger 10H (mountains + Sea of Cortez — $1,765), Extreme 16H (full Baja crossing — $2,695), and El Zorro 14 (PROBAJA race Can-Am circuit — $1,500).
