Baja Curated Expeditions

Cabo San Lucas sits at the tip of one of the most dramatic off-road landscapes on earth. Behind the marina, the resorts, and the tourist strips lies a Baja that most visitors never reach — volcanic desert, ancient arroyos, remote Pacific dunes, and a UNESCO World Heritage reef you can only access overland.

Baja Curated is the only off-road tour operator in Los Cabos that is also an Authorized Can-Am BRP Dealer. That means you drive a current-year 2025/2026 Maverick — factory-maintained, under full BRP warranty, fully insured — not a rental unit from a third party.

Every experience is 100% private. No shared groups, no waiting, no pace dictated by strangers. Your guide, your vehicle, your Baja.

FAQ

COMMON QUESTIONS

What are the best off-road tours in Cabo San Lucas?+
Baja Curated offers six private Can-Am Maverick experiences ranging from 3 to 16 hours, covering Baja desert trails, the East Cape coastline, and Cabo Pulmo UNESCO reef. As the only Authorized Can-Am BRP Dealer in Los Cabos, the fleet is always current-year and factory-maintained. Tours start at $550 USD.
Do I need experience to drive a Can-Am off-road in Cabo?+
No prior off-road experience is required. Every tour begins with a professional safety briefing and vehicle orientation. Our guides lead the route at a pace matched to your skill level. A valid driver's license is required.
How is Baja Curated different from other Cabo off-road tours?+
Three key differences: (1) You actually drive — most competitors offer ride-along experiences where a staff member operates the vehicle. (2) Authorized BRP dealer status means a certified, warranty-covered fleet. (3) Longer expedition routes that reach terrain no other operator accesses, including Cabo Pulmo UNESCO reef.
What is included in a Cabo off-road tour with Baja Curated?+
All tours include: 2025/2026 Can-Am Maverick, professional bilingual guide, full safety gear (helmet, harness, goggles, neck brace), in-vehicle and inter-vehicle radio communication, hydration throughout, pre-ride safety briefing, and Can-Am jersey. Longer tours include meals, snorkel equipment, and additional inclusions specific to each route.
Where do off-road tours depart from in Cabo San Lucas?+
Tours depart from a central meeting point in San José del Cabo or Cabo San Lucas, depending on the route. Hotel pickup is available for most experiences. WhatsApp us to confirm the exact departure point for your chosen tour.
